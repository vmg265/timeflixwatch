Frequently Asked Questions

Does it need a phone to work?

No, it works standalone. All core functionality like time, media, and health works without a phone.

How long does the battery last?

Up to 7 days with typical use. Longer with minimal smart features.

Can I install apps on it?

Yes! There's an app store and a developer kit to build your own apps and watchfaces.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. The device works offline and doesn’t force you to create an account. You own your data.

Is it waterproof?

Yes, it's 5ATM water resistant – safe for swimming and everyday wear.
